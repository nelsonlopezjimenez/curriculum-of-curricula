Guidelines:

Before you click on the hyperlink ("h-link") answer the question in your mind, or even better think for about five minutes and joggle your answer in a piece of paper (or better in a notebook, not on a piece of paper that might get lost, misplaced and forgotten). Then click on the hyperlink to visit the page, read, and at the end compare your written answer to what you just learned.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, students will be able to identify…

  • types of computers
  • different operating systems: PCs, Macs, Linux
  • operating systems for mobile devices: Apple iOS, Google Android
  • relations between programs, applications, and app
  • open source vs closed source software
