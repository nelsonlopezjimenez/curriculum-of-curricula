README Prerequisites Webdev Academic Program

What?

The content is about computer basics and about how the Internet works.

Materials presented here are mostly for offline viewing, taken from various publicly available sources and from materials graciously provided by other instructors teaching the same program on the main campus.

Some resources found on the Internet allowed to download the materials directly for offline viewing directly, or were scraped using httrack software.

The materials, as well as the flow of the presentation, are intended to be self contained, with no previous knowledge required, other than to be a regular user of a internet connected device (computer, mobile device or other). It does not require more than motivation to learn new things, interest in spending time learning, and willing to do it in an independent and self propelled way, with minimal interactions with others, just on the solitude of your own room in front of a laptop or tablet computer.

Who?

This compact course is directed to those interested in improving their technical and technological skills to be able to do more educated decisions regarding the acquisition and utilization of modern technologies. If you are invested in improving yourself by the way of technology, you must learn the basics of computer usage and the Internet. The knowledge you will acquire will assists you not just to make better decisions about your next laptop, or tablet, or cell phone, but also it will help you in case if you consider a professional path to follow, be it a career or a job in the tech world. 

How?

You will be presented with a content that can be studied in no particular order. You will decide where to start based on your own preferences. You have complete control on how much time are you willing to spend on any one topic. In case if you want to pursue further your education, then you must set a timeline and topics that would be directly related to your future plans. As an organizer of this presentation I will give suggestions based on the academic programs currently available.
